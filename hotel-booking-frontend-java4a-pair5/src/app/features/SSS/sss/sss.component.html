I was recently asked over the phone to pay using my gift card. Is this normal?
Booking.com will never ask you to share a credit card or gift card number over the phone. In fact, no matter the reason, if anyone asks you to pay with a gift card or share a credit card over the phone, it's likely a sign of fraud.
